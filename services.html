Frequently Asked Questions

How can I sign up for a membership?
To sign up for a membership, visit our gym and our friendly staff will assist you with the registration process.
What are your operating hours?
We are open from Monday to Saturday, 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays.
Do you offer personal training sessions?
Yes, we offer personalized training sessions with certified trainers to help you achieve your fitness goals.
Can I bring a guest to the gym?
We offer guest passes for visitors. Contact us for more information on guest policies and fees.
Are group fitness classes available?
Yes, we offer a variety of group fitness classes to cater to different preferences and fitness levels.
How can I cancel my membership?
Membership cancellation requests can be made in person at our front desk. Please refer to our membership policies for details.
